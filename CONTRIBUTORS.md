## How to Contribute

We welcome contributions from the community! Whether you're fixing bugs, adding features, improving documentation, or helping with testing, your contributions are valuable and appreciated.

To get started:

1. Read our [Contributing Guidelines](CONTRIBUTING.md)
2. Check out the [open issues](https://github.com/google/langextract/issues)
3. Submit a pull request

## Recognition

All contributors who have their pull requests merged into the project will be recognized here. Thank you for helping make LangExtract better!
